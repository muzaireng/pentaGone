Health Facility Registry
Introduction
Health Facility Registry (HFR) is valuable tool for improved care, reliable referral, better planning and accessible data of all types of health facilities. The HFR Form is designed by the Pak One Health Alliance (POHA) with the consultation of Provincial and District DHIS Managers to improve the Master Facility List as the official source of health facility data for the health care system. The HFR and the Master Facility List are part of a District Health Information System.

Punjab Information Technology Board (PITB) which is Punjab Provincial Government autonomous organization has already initiated a number of Public Health Projects in the Province of Punjab. To take advantage of technical expertise of PITB, POHA has now entered into a formal agreement for the Development and Launch of Geo enabled Health Facility Registry / Dashboard and other valuable Public Health related mobile applications.
